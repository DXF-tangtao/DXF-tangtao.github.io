关于我与本站 About Me & This Site

大家好，我是 Tang（唐），来自中国重庆。

Hello everyone, I’m Tang from Chongqing, China.

我是一个土生土长的重庆人，今年30岁，曾经是一名软件工程师，长期与代码、服务器和技术打交道。

I’m a native Chongqing local, 30 years old, and a former software engineer who spent years working with code, servers, and technology.

如今，我更想用真实、自然的方式，向世界分享我眼中的中国。

Today, I simply want to share the China I see in a real and natural way.

这里不仅有科技、城市与未来感，也有人情味、烟火气与普通人的生活。

Not only its technology, futuristic cities, and rapid development, but also its warmth, culture, and everyday life.

一座像“赛博朋克”一样的城市 A City That Feels Like Cyberpunk

我在重庆生活了二十多年。

I have lived in Chongqing for more than twenty years.

这是一座建在山脉与江河之间的巨大城市。

It is a massive city built between mountains and rivers.

在这里，你会看到：

In Chongqing, you will see:

  • 穿楼而过的轻轨
    Light rail trains passing directly through buildings

  • 层层叠叠的立体道路
    Multi-level roads stacked across mountains

  • 永远起伏的地形
    Endless hills and steep stairways

  • 夜晚照亮江面的霓虹灯
    Neon lights reflecting across the rivers at night

  • 让导航软件都“迷路”的复杂地形
    A city layout so complex that even navigation apps get confused

  • 深夜依旧热闹的火锅店与街边小吃
    Hotpot restaurants and street food crowded late into the night

很多外国游客第一次来到重庆时，都会惊叹：

Many foreign visitors say the same thing when they first arrive here:

“This looks like a real-life Cyberpunk city.”

而重庆最迷人的地方，并不仅仅是视觉上的未来感。

But Chongqing is not only about futuristic visuals.

这里既有现代科技与高速发展的城市节奏，也保留着最真实的市井生活与人情味。

Behind the skyscrapers and technology, there is still strong local culture, everyday life, and human warmth.

这种“未来感”与“烟火气”并存的感觉，正是我最喜欢重庆的原因。

That mix of futurism and real human life is exactly what fascinates me about Chongqing.

为什么创建这个网站 Why I Created This Website

这些年，我发现很多海外关于中国的内容，其实并不完整。

Over the years, I realized that much of the international content about China feels incomplete or outdated.

但真实的中国，其实每天都在快速变化。

The real China is changing incredibly fast every day.

在中国：

In modern China:

  • 移动支付已经成为日常
    Mobile payment is part of everyday life

  • 高铁连接着几乎所有大型城市
    High-speed rail connects most major cities

  • AI 技术正在进入普通人的生活
    AI technology is entering daily life

  • 无人配送与机器人服务越来越普遍
    Delivery robots and smart services are becoming common

  • 新能源汽车遍布街头
    Electric vehicles are everywhere

  • 城市正在不断变得更智能、更现代
    Cities are becoming smarter and more futuristic

所以，我创建了这个网站。

That is why I created this website.

我想通过真实的旅行、城市、科技与生活观察，让更多海外读者看到一个更真实、更现代、也更有人情味的中国。

I want overseas readers to discover a more authentic, modern, and human side of China through real travel experiences, technology, cities, and daily life.

不是刻板印象中的中国。

Not the stereotypical version of China.

而是真实存在、正在发生变化的中国。

But the real China that exists today.
